directions_carYour Personal Fuel Tracking App

FuelSense is your ultimate companion for tracking and optimizing your vehicle's fuel consumption. With our intuitive interface and powerful features, you can:

  • Log fuel fill-ups and track consumption
  • Monitor fuel costs and expenses
  • Analyze your carbon footprint
  • Get insights to improve fuel efficiency
  • Set reminders for maintenance and inspections

Take control of your fuel consumption and make informed decisions about your driving habits with FuelSense.

securityPrivacy Policy

At FuelSense, we prioritize your privacy and data security. Our commitment to you includes:

  • Collecting only essential information for app functionality
  • Secure storage and encryption of your data
  • No sharing of personal information with third parties
  • Transparent use of Google AdMob for advertising purposes

We strive to provide a safe and trustworthy environment for managing your fuel data.

settingsUser Privacy Options

We believe in giving you control over your data. With FuelSense, you can:

  • Choose what personal information to provide
  • Delete your data at any time within the app
  • Opt-out of personalized ads through your device settings
  • Request a copy of your data or its deletion

Your privacy is in your hands, and we're here to support your choices.

  • Yalnızca uygulama işlevselliği için gerekli bilgileri toplama
